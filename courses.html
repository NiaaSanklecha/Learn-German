How to Start your Bachelor’s or Master’s application with us?

Once, we are set in good communications with each other, you can send us scanned documents. Documents for University Applications - Bachelor/Master Studies:

  • Secondary school leaving certificate (10th & 12th grade)
  • University degree (Bachelor) transcripts and certificate, if any
  • Proficiency in German (required for courses taught in German)
  • Proficiency in English (TOEFL / IELTS – required for courses taught in English)
  • Copy of your passport
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)
  • Letter of motivation
  • Letter of recommendation
  • FOR MASTERS ONLY - Proof of successful studies (copies of list of subjects & grades (transcripts) for each semester studied in your home country completed at an accredited University)
